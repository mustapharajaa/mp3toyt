Data Deletion Instructions

LiveEnity uses Facebook Login to provide its core automation features. According to Facebook policy, we provide a way for users to request data deletion.

If you want to delete your data from the LiveEnity application, you can do so by following these steps:

Step 1: Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy" menu. Click "Settings".
Step 2: Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with your Facebook.
Step 3: Search and tap "LiveEnity" in the search bar.
Step 4: Scroll and tap "Remove".
Step 5: Congratulations, you have successfully removed your app activities and requested data deletion.

Alternatively, you can contact us directly at data@liveenity.com to request manual deletion of your account and associated data. Please include your Facebook user ID or the email associated with your account.
